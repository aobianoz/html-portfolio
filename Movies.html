The Best Movies or Shows of 2024-2025 According to Allin

My Top Ten Movies

  1. Devil May Cry Netflix Series

    2. Although it is not cannon to the origianal Devil May cry story this anime is a great adaptation to a iconic Capcom original character.

    8. Beware! This is not for children!!! Over the top action and gore!!!

    9. Another show that I couldn't put my finger on why I was drawn to it!!!

    10. Best movie I've seen about duty to family, betrayal, and prophecy!!!

    11. I am a big fan of Zack Snyder and I think Rebel Moon, uniquely, poses a forward fision in cinema quality.
